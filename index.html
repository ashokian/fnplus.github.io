Who are we?

We at Fnplus, trying to make India's largest student developer network. We help students to learn and develop real time professional projects for research and our valued clients. Training to be true professional developers and building a strong resume.

01.

Agile Training

We can't change the education system of India but we know the best way for students to learn. Fnplus is a complete student startup & with the help of our student trainers we have created a whole new virtual learning workspace with 24*7 support. Every aspirant of technology will have an individual trainer to guide throughout the development process.

02.

Research and Development

Student developers have potential to build something amazing. Our Research & Development department consists of some of the best student developers across India working together as a team. Fnplus R&D has developed many awesome products which are useful for society. If you are confident enough & have passion to build something, we will provide everything you need to turn your idea into reality.
